What is your typical project timeline?

Most Shopify store projects take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Custom theme development typically requires 3-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely! I offer various maintenance packages including 24/7 technical support, monthly updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization. Your store's success is my long-term priority.

Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?

Yes, I specialize in seamless migrations from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, and custom platforms. I ensure zero data loss, preserve your SEO rankings, and minimize downtime during the transition.

What makes your Shopify themes unique?

My themes are built from scratch with clean, optimized code. They feature lightning-fast load times, mobile-first responsive design, conversion-focused layouts, and easy customization options—all tailored to your brand identity.
