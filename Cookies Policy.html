02

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

Stored on Your Device

Cookies are stored in your browser's memory and help websites remember your preferences.

Secure & Safe

Our cookies don't contain personal information and cannot harm your device.

Session & Persistent

Some cookies expire when you close your browser, others stay for a set period.

First & Third Party

We use both our own cookies and trusted third-party cookies for analytics.

05

Managing Your Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by adjusting your browser settings. Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings.

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage cookies

Note: If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, but some functionality and features may be restricted or not work properly.
