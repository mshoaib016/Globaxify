Cookie Policy

We use cookies to personalize your experience and analyze our traffic to build a better Globaxify for you.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and ensure the website functions securely and efficiently.

2. How We Use Cookies

At Globaxify, we use cookies for the following purposes:

Type Purpose
Essential Required for the website to function (Security, Logins).
Analytical To understand how visitors interact with our site.
Marketing To show you relevant digital insights and services.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we use cookies provided by trusted third parties such as Google Analytics to help us understand how you use the site and ways that we can improve your experience.

4. Controlling Your Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience.

Your Choice Matters

By clicking "Accept", you agree to the storing of cookies on your device to enhance site navigation and site usage analysis.
